# Enigmatic & Nodal Business Context

## Enigmatic

Enigmatic is the parent company and service provider.
**Services:**

1. **Consulting**: Strategic advice on logistics operations and technology.
2. **Engineering**: Implementation and custom development services.

## Nodal

Nodal is the proprietary business process platform built by Enigmatic.

**Core Concept:**

- **Action Flows**: Workflows designed by technical engineers who understand the business context.
- **Action Types**:
  - **Workflow Action**: Automated steps executed by the system.
  - **AI Action**: Tasks performed by AI agents (e.g., document parsing, decision making).
  - **Human Action**: Tasks requiring human-in-the-loop intervention (e.g., review, approval, exception handling).
- **Designer Interface**: A visual tool resembling a mix of Lucidchart and automation platforms like n8n or Sim.ai.
- **Components**:
  - Automation Steps
  - Human-in-the-Loop Tasks
  - AI Agents

**Execution:**

- Workflows are triggered by specific events (webhooks, emails, status changes) or schedules.
- Execution involves a collaborative effort between the backend system, human operators, and AI agents.

**Value Proposition:**

- **Consistency**: Ensures business processes are followed strictly.
- **Validation**: Checks data and steps at every stage.
- **Automation**: Reduces manual effort where possible.
- **Flexibility**: Businesses can design their own processes, or Enigmatic can design them as a service.
